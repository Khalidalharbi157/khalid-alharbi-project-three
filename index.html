Epoch: (/ˈepək/) is a period of time in history or a person's life, typically one marked by notable events or particular characteristics. What era are we living in right now? Our existence and day to day life has become so muddled by fallacy that defining what is representative of now is impossible to say. Part of that comes from this being the age of physical attachment to imaginary objects. This series tries to define this era through surrealist documentary style photography.
