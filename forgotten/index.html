Why is it accepted that the only moments worth saving are the objectively “important” ones? Realistically those are the moments you will remember anyway, so why not try to save the times you will most likely forget? There is a lot of gold in those spaces in between; they deserve to be saved for later. What if you won’t even remember this right now?
